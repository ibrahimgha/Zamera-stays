Brief

1. Executive Overview

The proposed solution represents a governed digital investment platform designed to support the complete lifecycle of BAM's digital investment operations.

  • Customer-facing investment and savings capabilities
  • Operational governance and compliance interfaces
  • Financial processing and accounting oversight
  • Integration across internal and external systems
  • Regulatory compliance controls aligned with FRA requirements

The platform supports two currencies in Phase 1: Egyptian Pound (EGP) and US Dollar (USD).

1.1 Consumer Layer

  • Digital onboarding and e-KYC verification
  • Multi-channel wallet with cash-in and cash-out
  • Mutual fund subscription and redemption
  • Savings plans with goal tracking

1.2 Operational Governance

  • Workflow approvals
  • AML flag management
  • KYC updates and compliance monitoring
